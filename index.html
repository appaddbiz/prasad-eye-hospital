Surgeries

From blurry to brilliant—your vision, our care.

Phacoemulsification

Technique using ultrasound to break up and remove the lens.

Foldable IOLs

Foldable IOLs inserted through a small incision for vision.

Topical Anesthesia

Only eye drops are used for numbing, eliminating the need for injections.

Monofocal IOLs

Correct vision at a single distance (near or far).

Multifocal IOLs

Provide vision at multiple distances (near, intermediate, and far).

Toric IOLs

Corrects both cataracts and corneal irregularities for sharper vision.

SICS

Manual cataract removal with a small, self-sealing scleral incision.

Lasik

Laser reshapes cornea to correct vision and reduce glasses.

Epi-Lasik

Epithelium lifted, laser reshapes cornea for vision correction.

ICL

Implantable lens placed inside eye for high-power vision correction.

Glaucoma Surgeries

Procedures to reduce eye pressure and prevent optic nerve damage.

Trabeculectomy

Surgical procedure creates drainage channel to lower eye pressure.

Experience

26 Years Of Experience

Dr. Shiva Prasad Kakarla is a highly experienced cataract and refractive surgeon with 26 years of expertise. He has served as a senior consultant at renowned institutions, including Aravind Eye Hospital in Madurai, Sankara Eye Hospital in Coimbatore, and Bangalore West Lions Eye Hospital. Additionally, he has worked as a visiting Phaco surgeon at Marathas Hospital. Currently, Dr. Prasad is a consultant cataract and refractive surgeon at Prasad Eye Hospital and Modi Eye Hospital in Bangalore, while also performing freelance surgeries, specializing in Phacoemulsification under topical anesthesia. A high-volume cataract surgeon, he can efficiently operate on up to 50 cases in a single day and has performed over 40,000 cataract surgeries, along with 1,000 LASIK and ICL refractive procedures. Skilled in using both domestic and imported microscopes and phaco machines, he is also experienced in training residents in Phacoemulsification and managing surgical complications.
