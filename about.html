Dr. Shiva Prasad Kakarla completed his MBBS from M.R. Medical College, Gulbarga, followed by a DOMS from VIMS, Bellary. He further pursued a DNB in Ophthalmology at Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai, and specialized in anterior segment surgeries with a fellowship at Sankara Eye Hospital (1999-2000). His extensive training has built a strong foundation for his expertise in cataract and refractive surgeries.

Dr. Shiva Prasad Kakarla is a highly experienced cataract and refractive surgeon with 26 years of expertise. He has served as a senior consultant at renowned institutions, including Aravind Eye Hospital in Madurai, Sankara Eye Hospital in Coimbatore, and Bangalore West Lions Eye Hospital. Additionally, he has worked as a visiting Phaco surgeon at Marathas Hospital. Currently, Dr. Prasad is a consultant cataract and refractive surgeon at Prasad Eye Hospital and Modi Eye Hospital in Bangalore, while also performing freelance surgeries, specializing in Phacoemulsification under topical anesthesia. A high-volume cataract surgeon, he can efficiently operate on up to 50 cases in a single day and has performed over 40,000 cataract surgeries, along with 1,000 LASIK and ICL refractive procedures.

Surgeries Performed

At Prasad Eye Hospital, we specialize in advanced eye care procedures to ensure optimal vision and eye health. Our expert surgeons perform a wide range of surgical interventions using the latest technology and techniques to provide the best outcomes for our patients.

  • Phaco with Foldables Under Topical Anaesthesia, Monofocal, Multifocal, Toric IOLs
  • SICS (Small Incision Cataract Surgery)
  • Refractive Surgeries: LASIK, Epi-LASIK, ICL
  • Glaucoma Surgeries: Trabeculectomy
  • Phaco with Trabeculectomy
  • Pterygium Surgery with Conjunctival Grafting
  • Secondary Iris Claw Lenses
  • Astigmatic Correction
  • Corneal Transplants
  • Lid Surgeries
  • Laser Treatments, Medical Retina Procedures, and Intravitreal Injections
