Phacoemulsification

Technique using ultrasound to break up and remove the lens.

Foldable IOLs

Foldable IOLs inserted through a small incision for vision.

Topical Anesthesia

Only eye drops are used for numbing, eliminating the need for injections.

Monofocal IOLs

Correct vision at a single distance (near or far).

Multifocal IOLs

Provide vision at multiple distances (near, intermediate, and far).

Toric IOLs

Corrects both cataracts and corneal irregularities for sharper vision.

SICS

Manual cataract removal with a small, self-sealing scleral incision.

Lasik

Laser reshapes cornea to correct vision and reduce glasses.

Epi-Lasik

Epithelium lifted, laser reshapes cornea for vision correction.

ICL

Implantable lens placed inside eye for high-power vision correction.

Glaucoma Surgeries

Procedures to reduce eye pressure and prevent optic nerve damage.

Trabeculectomy

Surgical procedure creates drainage channel to lower eye pressure.

Pterygium with Conjunctival Grafting

Pterygium removed, healthy tissue grafted to prevent recurrence.

Secondary Iris Claw Lenses

Implantable lens clipped to iris for vision correction.

Astigmatic Correction

Reshaping cornea or using special lenses to correct astigmatism.

Conrneal Transplants

Replacing damaged cornea with healthy donor tissue for vision restoration.

Lid Surgeries

Correcting eyelid position, function, or appearance for better vision.
