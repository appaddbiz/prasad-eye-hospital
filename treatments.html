A Wide Range Of Eye Care Treatments.

Cataract and Refractive

Cataract and refractive services restore vision through advanced surgeries like IOL implantation, LASIK, and PRK, enhancing clarity and eye health.

Glaucoma

Glaucoma is a progressive eye disease that damages the optic nerve, causing vision loss, often without early symptoms, requiring timely treatment.

Read More

Vitreo-Retina & Uvea

Vitreo-Retina & Uvea Services restore vision by treating retinal disorders, uveitis, and macular diseases using advanced diagnostics, laser therapy.

Read More

Squint & Pediatric Ophthalmology

Diagnose and treat strabismus, amblyopia, and other childhood eye disorders, ensuring optimal visual development and alignment.

Read More

Corneal

Corneal services focus on diagnosing and treating corneal diseases, injuries, and infections. Treatments include transplants, laser procedures, and therapeutic care.

Services focus on reconstructive and cosmetic treatments for eyelids, tear ducts, and facial structures.
